#### **App Title: The GovTech Modernisation & Design Readiness Playbook**

#### **Target User: Senior Design Talent / Principal Designers**

#### **Core Logic Sections:**

**1\. The IMPACT Framework Audit (Strategy)**

* *Definition:* The systematic approach to untangling legacy government systems.  
* *Checklist Items:*  
  * **Business Fitness:** Does the product hinder policy implementation or frustrate citizens?  
  * **Technical Fitness:** Is the stack insecure, brittle, or incompatible with the SG Tech Stack (SGTS)?  
  * **Cost Fitness:** Are maintenance costs disproportionate to the value delivered?  
  * **Outcome over Feature Parity:** Are we building for better outcomes (reduced turnaround time) rather than just copying old features?

**2\. The AI & Engineering Productivity Layer (Efficiency)**

* *Context:* GovTech is moving toward "Agentic AI" and human-AI collaboration.  
* *Key Tools:*  
  * **Litmus:** Evaluating AI safety and reliability before go-live.  
  * **Sentinel:** Real-time content moderation and output filtering for live AI.  
  * **The "Commute Build" Standard:** Prioritizing rapid, intent-driven prototyping (vibe coding) to reduce iteration cycles by 20%+.

**3\. The Infrastructure Standard (Execution)**

* *Standards:*  
  * **SGDS (Design System):** Using plug-and-play components to ensure 100% WCAG 2.1 accessibility compliance.  
  * **SGTS (Tech Stack):** Leveraging shared modules like Singpass, Myinfo, and GovWallet for "national-scale" complexity.

**4\. The Value Proposition for Nav Pawera**

* **The Bridge:** Aceson brings healthtech rigor (MOH/MSF relevance) and enterprise scale (3,000+ users to 6M citizens).  
* **The Multiplier:** Proven experience mentoring junior designers and aligning PMs/Engineers.
