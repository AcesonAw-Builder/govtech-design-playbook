About This Playbook

Your guide to what's inside and why it matters

Government digital products touch millions of lives — but many run on systems that are outdated, expensive to maintain, and frustrating to use. This playbook is a practical reference for anyone involved in making those products better. It covers what to look for, which tools to use, and how to build things that last. Whether you're deeply technical or just getting oriented, start here.

📋

IMPACT Audit

Strategy — Know when to fix and when to rebuild

Government systems often keep running long past their usefulness — not because they work well, but because no one has paused to ask the right questions. The IMPACT Audit gives you a structured checklist to decide whether a system is worth fixing, rebuilding, or retiring. Use it at the start of any modernisation project to get everyone aligned.

AI & Engineering Layer

Efficiency — Speed with safety

AI is changing how digital products are built — but speed without safety can cause real harm in government services. This section covers the tools GovTech uses to make AI features reliable before they go live and safe while they're running. It also introduces a way of working that shortens the gap between an idea and a testable prototype.

Infrastructure Standard

Execution — Build on shared foundations

Singapore's government has shared building blocks — a design system and a technology stack — so no team starts from zero. They handle accessibility, security, and connections to national services like Singpass out of the box. This section explains what these standards are and why they're the right starting point for any new product.
